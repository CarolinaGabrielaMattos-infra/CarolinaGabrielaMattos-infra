The OMEGA-ULTRA® architecture is built on the principle of sovereign determinism. We take security seriously. If you discover a vulnerability or a potential leak in our hardened kernel configurations, we appreciate your professional cooperation in reporting it responsibly.

All proprietary logic, registry optimizations, and OMEGA-ULTRA® flow architectures are protected under Intellectual Property laws. ## 📩 Reporting a Vulnerability
Do **NOT** open a public Issue on GitHub for security-related findings.

To report a vulnerability, please follow this protocol:
1. **Encryption:** All communications must be professional and direct.
2. **Channel:** Send a detailed report to **carolina.mattos@mi.unc.edu.ar**.
3. **Response Time:** Our incident response team (Sovereign Node CBA) will acknowledge the receipt of your report within 48-72 business hours.
